# What is this about?

- This is a fictional organization that will be used in various scenarios throughout the training.
- Think of it as a case study to help you understand and apply the concepts being taught.

# About Animal4Life

- Animal4Life is a rescure and awareness organization
- Animal4Life is a global company, its HQ in Brisbane, Australia with 100 employees including:
  - Call center
  - Admin
  - Marketing
  - IT
  - Legals & Accounts
- There are about 100 remote workers around the world including:
  - Animal care workers
  - Vets
  - Researchers
- Animal4Life has major offices in:
  - London
  - New York
  - Seattle

# Technical Problems

- Small old on-premise data center in Brisbane.
- Badly implemented AWS trial in Sydney region.
- Have a few isolated Azure/GCP pilots.
- Global offices and mobile staff have to consume services from Brisbane data center. Some of the remote workers have very poor connectivity.
- Animal4life is very cost conscious, but also wants to try new technologies.
- Lack of high availability and scalability.
- Staff skills struggle, little automation.
- Global expansion concerns - cost for new infrastructure and complexity of new deployments.

# Ideal outcome

- Fast performance for all users globally.
- Able to deploy into new regions quickly and cost effectively.
- Low cost and scalable infrastructure.
- Agility: Spin up new marketing campaigns, progressive application (with new technologies).
- Automation: Low base staffing costs.
